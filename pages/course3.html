Data Analytics & Business Intelligence

Course Objective

This program aims to equip final-year students with essential skills in Data Analytics and Business Intelligence, enabling them to analyze, interpret, and present data effectively. Through hands-on training in Python, MySQL, Advanced Excel, and Power BI, students will learn data cleaning, transformation, visualization, and storytelling. By completing real-world projects, they will gain the technical and analytical expertise needed to excel in roles such as Data Analyst or Business Intelligence Specialist in a data-driven industry.

Course Modules

Module 1: Introduction to Data Analytics and Python for Data Analysis

Foundations of Data Analytics, Data Visualization, and Python Basics for Data Analytics

Module 2: Relational Databases and SQL for Analytics

Learn how to use MySQL to store, query, and manage data.

Module 3: Advanced Excel for Data Analysis

This module focuses on Excel techniques commonly used in industry

Module 4: Power BI for Business Intelligence

Master Power BI for interactive data visualizations and dashboards

Course Outcome

By the end of this program, students will be equipped with the technical skills and practical knowledge to excel in data analytics and business intelligence roles. They will be proficient in Python, MySQL, Advanced Excel, and Power BI, enabling them to clean, analyze, and visualize data effectively. Students will also gain experience in creating interactive dashboards, performing advanced SQL queries, and generating insights to drive business decisions, preparing them for roles like Data Analyst or BI Specialist and other related roles.
